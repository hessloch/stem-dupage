Places To Go

Cool Places To Go for STEM Fun!

Talk to your parents or friends about planning an outing to one of Chicago's fun and inspirational STEM spots. Perhaps your aunt, uncle and cousins would like to go with you.

Check out these websites for days and hours of operation, admission fees, as well as special exhibits and programs.
