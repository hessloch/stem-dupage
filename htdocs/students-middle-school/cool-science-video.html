How a Tesla is Made

About This Video:

The term robot was first used by the writer Karel Capek in a 1920 theatre presentation. Robota means "forced labor" in Czech. The idea of robots helping man has a much longer history, though. A google search will lead you to a fascinating story. This video shows the sleek and premium electric car, Tesla, being made by what else, robots!
