About STEM DuPage

STEMDuPage has been developed as a resource for residents of our county and beyond, by the STEM Team at the DuPage Regional Office of Education. The website idea originated from a community advisory committee and was built with assistance from the DuPage Workforce Board. Visit their Website

Our goals are to provide information, encourage connection and collaboration, and motivate learning and play in the areas of Science Technology Engineering and Mathematics.

Susan Camasta, of the STEM Team, is the content manager of this website. You can contact Susan at stem@dupageroe.org.
