STEM on Presidents' Day!

Wind power, DNA, the energy bike, tree rings, fingerprints and more; nearly 200 parents and kids explored exhibits as they had some STEM fun at our annual Presidents’ Day event.  And if a morning of fun wasn’t enough, there were fantastic prizes that each child received to continue the fun at home.

A great big thank you to TCD for hosting our event, and kudos as well to our exhibitors:
